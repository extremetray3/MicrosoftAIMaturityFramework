It requires a partner who can help with strategy, solution development, platform engineering, security, and organizational change. **Microsoft Industry Solutions Delivery (ISD)** works globally with customers every day, across different industries, co-architecting and building AI solutions that solve real problems.

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:puzzle-piece" width="24" height="24" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.2em;"></iconify-icon> How It Comes Together

These three layers align to Microsoft's solution areas — **AI Business Solutions**, **Cloud and AI Platforms**, **Security**, and **AI Transformation** — working in concert rather than as silos:

- **AI Business Solutions + AI Transformation** experts identify the right value patterns, redesign processes, and build the solutions
- **Cloud and AI Platform** engineers ensure the technical architecture can scale
- **Security and governance** specialists embed trust, compliance, and observability into every AI deployment

At the center of the execution model is the **two-speed AI strategy**: speed one builds momentum and broad adoption; speed two builds durable, industrial-grade intelligence. In practice, these speeds run in parallel — delivering quick wins without losing sight of long-term architecture and scale.

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:warning" width="24" height="24" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.2em;"></iconify-icon> Addressing Common Barriers

<div markdown="0">
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/frontier/biggest-challenges.png" alt="Biggest Challenges" style="max-width: 100%; border-radius: 8px; margin: 1rem 0 1.5rem;" />
</div>

Across customer engagements, the most consistent challenges include:

| Barrier | Description |
|:--------|:-----------|
| **Unclear value story & ROI** | Projects are technology-led rather than outcome-led |
| **Missing AI operating model** | No consistent approach to move from idea to production at scale |
| **Data, security & governance gaps** | Treated as afterthoughts, leading to inconsistency and trust deficits |
| **Fragmented AI projects** | High cost per use case and limited production impact |

Microsoft ISD helps organizations systematically resolve each barrier — connecting AI investment directly to business KPIs, establishing cross-enterprise "AI factory" operating models, embedding governance from day one, and consolidating fragmented efforts into coherent, reusable platforms.

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:brain" width="24" height="24" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.2em;"></iconify-icon> Intelligence Layers

Microsoft has created capabilities specifically designed to address the semantic and observability foundations that Frontier Firms require:

| Layer | Purpose |
|:------|:--------|
| **Work IQ** | Understanding how people actually work |
| **Fabric IQ** | Providing a shared semantic model across the enterprise |
| **Foundry IQ** | Grounding AI agents in the right knowledge |

These intelligence layers ensure that every agent, copilot, and workflow operates from the same understanding of the business. As AI scales, so does confidence in the outputs.

**Enterprise-grade agent management** provides full observability across every agent's actions, decisions, and data access — combined with guardrails and human-in-the-loop controls.
