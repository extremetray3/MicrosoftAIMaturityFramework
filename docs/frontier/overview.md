# <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:rocket" width="36" height="36" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.3em;"></iconify-icon> Becoming a Frontier Firm with Microsoft ISD Services

AI has fundamentally changed the strategic conversation. It has moved from innovation labs and isolated pilots to the top of the boardroom agenda, shaping workforce strategies, capital allocation, and operating model redesign. Leaders are no longer asking *whether* AI will transform their business. They are asking **how fast the organization can adapt** — and **where to unlock value next**.

A new class of organization is emerging in response: **the Frontier Firm**.

---

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:magnifying-glass-tilted-left" width="24" height="24" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.2em;"></iconify-icon> What Is a Frontier Firm?

<div markdown="0">
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/frontier/frontier-definition.png" alt="What is a Frontier Firm?" style="max-width: 100%; border-radius: 8px; margin: 1rem 0;" />
</div>

A Frontier Firm is one where human and AI agents collaborate, and business value is unlocked through measurable, scalable AI innovation. It is not defined by a single tool or a handful of pilot projects. It is defined by an **enterprise-wide commitment** to treat AI as a strategic capability — not an add-on.

<div markdown="0">

| Characteristic | Description |
|:--|:--|
| **Organization-wide AI deployment** | AI is scaled across the enterprise and becomes part of how the company operates, not a siloed departmental initiative |

| **Agent–human collaboration at scale** | AI agents reason, take action, and work alongside humans as collaborative teammates to accelerate outcomes |

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:check-mark-button" width="24" height="24" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.2em;"></iconify-icon> The Frontier Is Here

Becoming a Frontier Firm means evolving into an AI-powered enterprise where technology and people operate together at a new level of performance. It is both an aspirational vision and a pragmatic roadmap — employees supercharged with copilots, customers enjoying intelligent services, processes running with unprecedented efficiency, and innovation accelerating beyond the usual curve.

Every organization's path will look different. Some will start by boosting productivity through workforce-wide AI tools; others will lead with a bold move on a flagship process or a new AI-driven product. But to reach Frontier status, **all dimensions — people, processes, technology, and governance — must be addressed in an integrated way**.

The dual lens of transformation pillars and maturity phases provides the framework: the **pillars** define what needs to change; the **phases** guide how to get there.

Microsoft ISD Services brings the experience of real-world AI transformations across industries and geographies to help you avoid pitfalls and accelerate success. **The Frontier is here. With the right strategy and the right partnership, you can seize it today.**
